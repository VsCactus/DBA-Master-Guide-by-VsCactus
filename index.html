⌨ Movement & Macros
Movement

Arrow Keys: Left=West | Up=North | Right=East | Down=South. Combine 2 for diagonals.
Numpad (Num Lock OFF): 1=SW   2=S   3=SE   4=W   6=E   7=NW   8=N   9=NE

Macro Setup

Commands (top-left) → Set Macros
Multi-word: use hyphen → ki-blast
.click z-sword = equip/unequip without inventory
.click gi\nhide gi = equip + hide in one key

Skill Bar & Skillcards

Techniques use the Skill Bar and Skillcard GUI. Skillcards appear at the bottom of your screen.

Assign: Click an empty slot → select technique
Remove: Click an occupied slot → Remove
Use: Press 1–9 for the corresponding slot
Target: Spacebar  |  Untarget: U

Keys 1–9, Spacebar, and U are hard-coded. Delete any macros bound to these keys to avoid conflicts.

📊 Stats & Abilities
Core Stats
  • Powerlevel (PL): Reaches 0 = KO/killed. Health/durability only
  • Ki: Strength of ki attacks
  • Ki Defense: Defence vs. ki attacks
  • Strength: Strength of melee attacks
  • Defense: Defence vs. melee attacks
  • Fatigue (Stamina): How long you can train. Depletes during training and gym equipment use. Restore with treadmills
Abilities (talk to Hercule | cap: 75%)
  • Block: Chance to block melee attacks
  • Counter: Chance to counter melee attacks
  • Critical: Chance to score a critical hit
  • Dodge: Chance to dodge ki attacks
  • Reflect: Chance to reflect ki attacks

☯ Karma System

Meditate to shift your karma toward Good or Evil. Karma direction matters for some NPC quests and stat boosts (e.g. Guru requires 25% Good Karma, Babidi requires 100% Evil). You also gain karma by killing mobs or players of the opposite alignment.

💀 Hakai

Hakai obliterates the target and sends them to jail for 30 minutes. Used to pause another player's progression. Has a 1 day cooldown. The jailed player cannot log out while the timer is active, or the timer resets.

  • Purchase Hakai: Z Agent (costs ZP)
  • Hakai Safety: Granted automatically for 2 hours after leaving jail. Can also be purchased at the Z Agent
  • Reset Hakai Cooldown: Z Agent (ZP) or Daily Quest Rewards Store (10 tokens)

Do NOT boost yet: save your boost for the End of Wipe tournament. Candy farm to start mobbing SR portals (candy gains are much higher at 5/3). Enter events, especially Mystical Cavern and Grand Maze for club tickets. You'll need a lot of ZP for level caps and TNL cuts.

3
Level 500,000: Sell Down & Build Stats
Sell 200k–300k levels for zenni (2b–3b) at the Exchanger NPC; sell the rest for ZP at Z Agent. Mob between Level 35,000–100,000 for optimal gains. Can also mob 100k–200k and sell for zenni to get house equipment faster. Cut TNL at 40m (30m if Universe GoD).
4
Portal Maze & Endgame Mobbing
At 1m TL: start mobbing Portal Maze. At 2 trillion stats: clear Lab 4 and Champion Tower (and replications via Zeno NPC). Best EXP at this stage. Push toward Lab 5 and ZR Portal as stats allow.
5
Stat Building & Equipment
At 30b+ base stats: kill Golden Frieza in Mystical Cavern for Cavern Pill (increased stat gains per level; always use while mobbing). Buy Legendary equipment from Real Estate Renji (10b zenni each). Unlock mob floors in your house for extra farm each repop.
6/3 Once you've mastered 5/3, the next frontier is 6/3 via Beerus. You'll need Level 500,000 and must complete 3 trials (Instruction, Endurance, Growth). Grants a +30% EXP boost and a choice of powerful path: TPs, Gym, or Mob Farming. See the Rebirth System section below for full details.
🏠 Housing

Walk up to any available house and buy it. Houses include an open area for equipment and decorations, a gravity room, and a mob room (unlock additional mob floors as you progress).

  • Cost: 2,500 UP at 5/3
  • Legendary House Equipment: Buy from Real Estate Renji NPC (10b zenni per piece). Best gym equipment outside of Club House
  • Farm your mob floors each repop for extra EXP alongside your regular mobbing rotation
Why Push to 5/3?

At 5/3 you unlock Zeno races (super strong races with special skills), Ultra Forms, AND Dual Gene!

  • Semi-perm/perm races → Ultra Invert
  • Non-perm races → Ultra Instinct or Ultra Ego
  • Non-perms can change Ultra Form at Oracle Fish for 10,000 ZP: choose carefully, you'll be stuck with it for a while
  • Ultra Forms work like UT. Put Ultra Points into your multis

💰 Currencies
Zenni

Main currency; transferable between players.
Sources: mobbing, quests, selling items, mall lottery, events.

Banker NPC: Withdraw/Deposit. Can transfer banked zenni to other players.

Used at Z Agent, special suit store, sword vendor. Critical for progression.
Z Agent ZP Skill Trees gated behind 5/3 rebirths.

UP (Universe Points)

Used at Agent X (Level 250k+, orange portal by Zeno). Buy house at 5/3: 2,500 UP.
Sources: Conquest, Tournament of Power, Pillar, Vegeta quest.

Agent X: Convert UP → ZP, GP, or TPs (see rates below).

TL (True Level)

Required for Oracle Fish Ultra Points & Dual Gene (10.5m TL threshold).

Oracle Fish: Exchange TL for Ultra Points (1m TL = 1 UP).

EK (Epic Kills)

Sources: sagas, portal mobs, tower mobs, Champion Tower, Lab 4.

Oracle Fish / Hunter NPC: Exchange for TPs. Broker NPC (Mall): Exchange for ZP.

📊 Race Tier List [4/3] (by Aleister)
SS
Gogeta, Vegito, Omega Shenron, Broly Black, Granolah
These have dominated for a long time. Gogeta/Vegito sit at the top together. Gogeta is better for PvP, Vegito more popular due to STR-based mobbing. Omega: best semi-perm in the game. Broly Black: does everything Broly does + Kai perks + Android techs. Granolah: very strong techs, kit too good to ignore.
S
Android 21, Broly, Gohan, Jiren
A21: massive multi, access to all Android techs. Broly: Wrathful Spirit (2x all stats) extremely valuable; Blaster Shell+BBA in 2-tile PvP. Gohan: Z-Sword buffs + BBA + Saiyan techs; above Broly for Z-Sword. Jiren: highest-damage homing in game, powerful AoE, extremely Ki-focused.
A
Red Eyed Namekian, Fu, Cauliflower Saiba, Super 17
Strong and viable choices. Red Eyed has extremely high multis (after S tier consideration). Fu (similar multis to Bojack but better with Stone Spit + Time Skip). Super 17 benefits heavily from being Android with access to all Android techs.
B
Bojack, Herculean, Cooler
Solid but nothing special. Bojack: top of B due to stun + Stone Spit (homing). Herculean + Cooler: one is Ki-scaled, other is STR-scaled; pair that amplifies race techs.
C
Yadrat Trapper, Beerus, Champa, Orange Piccolo, Garlic, Gotenks
