📊 Stats & Abilities
Core Stats
  • Powerlevel (PL): Reaches 0 = KO/killed. Health/durability only
  • Ki: Strength of ki attacks
  • Ki Defense: Defence vs. ki attacks
  • Strength: Strength of melee attacks
  • Defense: Defence vs. melee attacks
Abilities (talk to Hercule | cap: 75%)
  • Block — Chance to block melee attacks
  • Counter — Chance to counter melee attacks
  • Critical — Chance to score a critical hit
  • Dodge — Chance to dodge ki attacks
  • Reflect — Chance to reflect ki attacks

🏁 Road to 5/3 Champion
0/3 → 1/3 (Level 1 → 50,000)
1
Level 1 → 10,000
Pick Velocitor as your class. Race doesn't really matter as long as it's not a race with permanent transformations — they have a harder time early and are not advised. Talk to Yamcha, get to Level 500, talk to him again, then leave the house. If you have a season pass or a sub, use one of those boosts (preferably season pass — it also gives zenni). Use that zenni to buy ultra weights. Also buy a small EXP Shard if you have the zenni. Get Kaioken from King Kai and go to Master Roshi's Training Island or the Orange Star portal (right side of map). Only mob Crocodiles and Wolves. Be sure to use an EXP shard and Weights to boost your mobbing EXP. Another option is to go to sub house gravity or HBTC (must fly to Yajirobe if you don't have IT unlocked — IT can be unlocked at Level 2,000 by talking to Yamcha and clicking Learn Teleport). Either self train, dual train, or shadow spar. Shadow spar is fastest if you can keep it at x3 often. If you have a partner you can dual train instead.
2
Level 10,000 → 20,000
Once you hit Level 10,000 you can start doing sims inside safe zone house. Go down the stairs to the right, go past Hercule and down the 2nd set of stairs, once you're in the sim room access a computer, right click (or make a macro for it) and start spawning clones to farm. The best way to farm them is to power down to PL 1 and as soon as you spawn the clone, hold your attack macro and repeat. Macro to start clone → Enter → Hold attack macro → Repeat. This will roughly get you to around Level 20k if you're using weights and EXP shard/happy meals.
3
Level 20,000 → 40,000
From here we strongly recommend Focus Meditation and Shadow Spar the Focus Med to speed up the process. If you're fast enough you can complete it in roughly 5 or 6 hours. There really aren't that many options to mob with where you'll be stat wise.
4
Level 40,000 → 50,000
Once you're at 40,000 / finished your Focus Med, make sure you turn in your Focus Med quest to Roshi for your rewards. Use any TPs you have to evenly distribute your stats (except Ki). Since you're now at the level threshold you can boost your stats and start mobbing things like Glass Civilian Cave, Xeno Pan, Copy Vegetas, Universe Caves on Beerus Planet, Tuffle Civilian 2-4, the other 3 Space Portals (not Orange Star), Portal NE of Vegito (Center of Cell Arena in Future Earth), and Android Caves NW of Vegito until you're ready to Rebirth.
5
Lazy/AFK Alternative: Level 2,000 → 50,000
At Level 2,000 talk to Yamcha to get IT, then obtain your 5+ quest medals, make an alt or find someone to dual train with in HBTC using your weights (preferably ultra weights) and EXP shard until you reach 25,000. From here, go talk to Master Roshi to unlock Focus Meditation (unlocked at Level 10,000 — we do it at 25,000 so we can rebirth as soon as Focus Med is done). After talking to him, he gives you the key to his house. Go inside, hop on a pad and start Focus Meditating. Focus Meditation takes 16 hours to complete and gives you ZP, a club ticket, and more. People advise doing this every rebirth because of the ZP you will need a lot of later. The only downside is you pretty much sit AFK for 16 hours every rebirth.
R
Rebirth at Level 50,000
Once you hit Level 50,000 you can rebirth by talking to King Kai or Dr. Gero depending on your race. Now you can choose another race to rebirth into and select a gene if they have any. Keep in mind you cannot select the same race you currently are. Once again, try not to pick a race that has permanent transformations. Do not forget to do ZP quests before you rebirth and to put all your items in a locker above the Yamcha NPC!
0/3 → 3/3 (Level 75,000 at 1/3, Level 100,000 at 2/3)

Simply follow the same steps as previous rebirth until you hit 3/3 Rebirth. You should be able to start mobbing earlier with each successive rebirth since you get more stats per rebirth. The only difference is that at 1/3 you need to hit Level 75,000 instead of 50,000, and at 2/3 you need Level 100,000.

(Optional) Before you rebirth from 1/3 to 2/3, don't forget to do Whis' quest for Whis weights and the second quest. The easiest way to complete the second quest is to go to Zeno, ask him to show you his powers, and do his tower replicas.

Do not forget to do ZP quests before you rebirth and to put all your items in a locker above the Yamcha NPC!

3/3 → 4/3 (Level 1 → 150,000)
1
Level 1 → 100,000
(For 3/3) You follow the same steps as before, however this time you cannot boost until you have the stats to boost AND be able to kill the portal boss. You need 150 million stats minimum before boosting (300m recommended weighted).
2
Level 100,000 → 150,000
Once you hit Level 100,000, it is time to buy your large shard. You 100% need to buy a large EXP shard. The Large Zenni shard can wait, so buy it later. Now you can purchase your first couple of Powerlevel candies and go into the Club Frog room and farm frogs (North of the white grave area). The other stats can be trained by using equipment — it doesn't take too long. You can kill portal bosses by having 150 million stats minimum. 300 million is recommended if you want to mob them weighted, but don't worry about it too much. You can farm regular portal mobs and get to Level 150,000 quite easily. You can also only focus on your PL and STR and boost that way, but you will be reverting/transforming and dying quite a lot when mobbing — but can still be easily done.
R
Rebirth to 4/3
You can rebirth once you have hit Level 150,000 and killed one of the SR portal bosses. You also need to have used the Super Dragon Balls once. You cannot rebirth unless your super wish says 1/1 in your skills tab (DO NOT RESET IT UNTIL YOU REBIRTH — YOU WILL NOT BE EDITED). When using Super Dragon Balls be sure to wish for ZP. It gives you a lot and you will need it. It is not advised to wish for anything else. If you wished for ZP you can buy a Pride Troopers Suit or Dark Robe, the choice is yours. Pride Troopers is useful early and mid game; Dark Robe is better late game. If you're short ZP, save your ZP for level cap increases if you intend to reach Champion at 5/3. To rebirth you have to talk to the NPC inside the SR portal of your choice. Do not forget to do ZP quests before you rebirth and to put all your items in a locker above the Yamcha NPC!
4/3 → 5/3 (Level 1 → 250,000)
1
Level 1 → 250,000
As with previous rebirths, you mostly follow the same steps. This time it is heavily advised to boost at 300–350 million ALL stats before boosting to farm SR portal mobs and bosses for efficient EXP, since leveling to 250,000 can take some time. This rebirth you also most likely will use a TNL cut or more, depending on how much ZP you can afford to spend. It is recommended to only use one TNL cut around Level 190,000–200,000. If you do not want to spend ZP for TNL cuts then you can still mob till Level 250,000 but it takes more time. Another way would be to get the Level crystals in Ice/Fire cave but it usually takes longer, depending if there is an EXP event active or not.
R
Rebirth to 5/3
To rebirth to 5/3 you must get to Level 250,000 and complete Zeno's quest. The Zeno quest has you kill ALL SR portal bosses. When you talk to him again he'll give you the list of bosses you need to kill. Once all required bosses have been killed, talk to him to rebirth. Do not forget to do ZP quests before you rebirth and to put all your items in a locker above the Yamcha NPC!
5/3 — Level 1 → 500,000 & End Game
1
Level 1 → 500,000
As always everything you do will be the same except you will not be boosting at all. You will boost before the End of Wipe tournament. This means you need to candy farm lots to start mobbing SR portals — but don't worry, it will not take long. Candy gains always increase with each rebirth, meaning at 5/3 your gains are a lot higher. Do not forget to enter events, especially Mystical Cavern and Grand Maze for club tickets. All the ZP will be needed as well for level caps and TNL cuts.
2
Buy ALL Level Caps Before TNL Cuts
TNL cutting will now cost you 400 ZP instead of 500 ZP and can be used unlimited times. TNL cutting should be done around 60 million TNL before Level 350,000. After Level 350,000, TNL cutting should be around 100 million TNL (it all depends on how much ZP you have at this point — if you have a lot, you can cut earlier). If you're short on ZP, you can also exchange your UP at Agent X in the Zeno portal. However it's advised NOT to exchange UP for ZP and buy a house instead, as the exchange rate is 100:25 UP to ZP. The faster you get your house the better. If you're still short, farm EK from portals or towers and exchange them for ZP — the rate is 2,500 EK for 200 ZP by talking to the Broker NPC inside the Mall.
3
DBA Life After Level 500,000
Congratulations, you've hit Level 500,000! Now it's time to sell down your levels for zenni and ZP by talking to Exchanger NPC and Z Agent. It's advised to sell at least 200,000 or 300,000 levels for zenni (which gives you 2b or 3b zenni) and the rest for ZP. That all depends on how much ZP you have since you'll be spending ZP for TNL cuts.
4
Portal Maze & End Game Mobbing
To start mobbing Portal Maze, you need 1 million TL. It is recommended once you reach 1m TL to mob here until you can clear the higher end areas. To start mobbing Lab 4 and Champion Tower and their replications, you need roughly 2 trillion stats to clear everything (except Ace and Shredz). You can enter replications by talking to Zeno NPC. Mobs there give the most EXP at this point — that's where you'll be mobbing until you can mob Lab 5 or ZR Portal.
5
Stat Building & Equipment
Once your base stats are 30b or higher, you can kill Golden Frieza in the Mystical Cavern crystal room. Frieza has a chance to drop a Cavern Pill which increases your stats gained per level. This is the pill you always want to use when mobbing. When you have a house, buy Legendary equipment from Real Estate Renji NPC — each piece costs 10 billion zenni. Also unlock your other two mob floors in your house which you can farm each repop after you do your recommended mobbing rotation. When mobbing, you always want to mob between Level 35,000–100,000. At first you can also mob between 100k–200k and sell down levels for zenni to get your house equipment faster. You can also cut your TNL every time it hits 40 million (30 million if you're a Universe GoD).
Why Should I Hit 5/3?

You may be asking yourself, why would I ever want to hit 5/3 if I get a powerful super race at 4/3? Well, at 5/3 you also get access to the Zeno races which are super strong races with special skills. Additionally you also get access to Ultra Forms AND Dual Gene!

  • If you're a semi-perm/perm race, you ONLY get access to Ultra Invert
  • If you're a non-perm race, you get access to either Ultra Instinct or Ultra Ego
  • Non-perms are able to change their Ultra Form at Oracle Fish for 10,000 ZP, so choose very carefully on what ultra you want to proceed with as you will be stuck with that ultra for a while
  • Ultra Forms work similar to UT in the aspect that you're able to put Ultra Points into your multis

📊 Race Tier List — 4/3 (by Aleister)
SS
Gogeta, Vegito, Omega Shenron, Broly Black, Granolah
These have dominated for a long time. Gogeta/Vegito sit at the top together — Gogeta better for PvP, Vegito more popular due to STR-based mobbing. Omega: best semi-perm in the game. Broly Black: does everything Broly does + Kai perks + Android techs. Granolah: very strong techs, kit too good to ignore.
S
Android 21, Broly, Gohan, Jiren
A21: massive multi, access to all Android techs. Broly: Wrathful Spirit (2x all stats) extremely valuable; Blaster Shell+BBA in 2-tile PvP. Gohan: Z-Sword buffs + BBA + Saiyan techs; above Broly for Z-Sword. Jiren: highest-damage homing in game, powerful AoE, extremely Ki-focused.
A
Red Eyed Namekian, Fu, Cauliflower Saiba, Super 17
Strong and viable choices. Red Eyed has extremely high multis (after S tier consideration). Fu (similar multis to Bojack but better with Stone Spit + Time Skip). Super 17 benefits heavily from being Android with access to all Android techs.
B
Bojack, Herculean, Cooler
Solid but nothing special. Bojack: top of B due to stun + Stone Spit (homing). Herculean + Cooler: one is Ki-scaled, other is STR-scaled; pair that amplifies race techs.
C
Yadrat Trapper, Beerus, Champa, Orange Piccolo, Garlic, Gotenks
Just okay. No special tech that sets them apart. Missing special techs that could rank them higher.
D
Any ZR Race; anything that is a full perm or has a permanent last trans
Full perms are annoying to mob with — no way to reliably regenerate PL once it's lost. The only real options are healing items.
HIT
Hit — Worst unit in the game. PvP race with multis that fall off very quickly. Needs a massive overhaul to be viable.
