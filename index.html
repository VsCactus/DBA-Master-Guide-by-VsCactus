⌨ Movement & Macros
Movement

Arrow Keys: Left=West | Up=North | Right=East | Down=South. Combine 2 for diagonals.
Numpad (Num Lock OFF): 1=SW · 2=S · 3=SE · 4=W · 6=E · 7=NW · 8=N · 9=NE

Macro Setup

Commands (top-left) → Set Macros. Multi-word: use hyphen → ki-blast
.click z-sword = equip/unequip without inventory · .click gi\nhide gi = equip + hide in one key

Skill Bar & Skillcards

Techniques use the Skill Bar and Skillcard GUI. Skillcards appear at the bottom of your screen.

Assign: Click an empty slot → select technique · Remove: Click an occupied slot → Remove · Use: Press 1–9 for the corresponding slot · Target: Spacebar · Untarget: U

Keys 1–9, Spacebar, and U are hard-coded. Delete any macros bound to these keys to avoid conflicts.

📊 Stats & Abilities
Core Stats
  • Powerlevel (PL): Reaches 0 = KO/killed. Health/durability only
  • Ki: Strength of ki attacks
  • Ki Defense: Defence vs. ki attacks
  • Strength: Strength of melee attacks
  • Defense: Defence vs. melee attacks
  • Fatigue (Stamina): How long you can train. Depletes during training and gym equipment use. Restore with treadmills
Abilities (talk to Hercule | cap: 75%)
  • Block: Chance to block melee attacks
  • Counter: Chance to counter melee attacks
  • Critical: Chance to score a critical hit
  • Dodge: Chance to dodge ki attacks
  • Reflect: Chance to reflect ki attacks

Why Push to 5/3?

At 5/3 you unlock Zeno races (super strong races with special skills), Ultra Forms, AND Dual Gene!

  • Ultra Forms work like UT. Put Ultra Points into your multis

📊 Race Tier List [4/3] (by Aleister)
SS
Gogeta, Vegito, Omega Shenron, Broly Black, Granolah
These have dominated for a long time. Gogeta/Vegito sit at the top together. Gogeta is better for PvP, Vegito more popular due to STR-based mobbing. Omega: best semi-perm in the game. Broly Black: does everything Broly does + Kai perks + Android techs. Granolah: very strong techs, kit too good to ignore.
S
Android 21, Broly, Gohan, Jiren
A21: massive multi, access to all Android techs. Broly: Wrathful Spirit (2x all stats) extremely valuable; Blaster Shell+BBA in 2-tile PvP. Gohan: Z-Sword buffs + BBA + Saiyan techs; above Broly for Z-Sword. Jiren: highest-damage homing in game, powerful AoE, extremely Ki-focused.
A
Red Eyed Namekian, Fu, Cauliflower Saiba, Super 17
Strong and viable choices. Red Eyed has extremely high multis (after S tier consideration). Fu (similar multis to Bojack but better with Stone Spit + Time Skip). Super 17 benefits heavily from being Android with access to all Android techs.
B
Bojack, Herculean, Cooler
Solid but nothing special. Bojack: top of B due to stun + Stone Spit (homing). Herculean + Cooler: one is Ki-scaled, other is STR-scaled; pair that amplifies race techs.
C
Yadrat Trapper, Beerus, Champa, Orange Piccolo, Garlic, Gotenks
Just okay. No special tech that sets them apart. Missing special techs that could rank them higher.
D
Any ZR Race; anything that is a full perm or has a permanent last trans
Full perms are annoying to mob with since there's no way to reliably regenerate PL once it's lost. The only real options are healing items.
HIT
Hit: Worst unit in the game. PvP race with multis that fall off very quickly. Needs a massive overhaul to be viable.

💡 Major in-game buildings and locations. Each has unique NPCs, equipment, or activities.
🎰 Casino

Located behind the Mall Mascot. Walk down the carpet to find two main rooms: the standard Casino room and the Sub Casino room.

  • Games include slot machines, blackjack, and more
  • Earn Casino Tokens (C) by playing, then spend them at the Casino Shop or Oolong's NPCs
  • Oolong's Exchange: Convert ZP and UP into Casino Tokens
  • Oolong's Lottery: Buy lotto tickets (automatically redeems)
  • Casino Manager: All casino rules, leaderboard, and the Black Book

🎪 Club (Club House)

A premium gravity training location with the highest multiplier available.

  • Gravity: x30 multiplier
  • Access: Requires a Club Ticket (purchase from Z Agent for ZP)
  • Session Duration: 30 minutes (45 minutes with Gym Bunny race)
🏋️ FCC (Future Capsule Corp.)

Located on Future Earth. A special training facility unlocked through Gogeta's quest.

  • Gravity Room: High-level gravity training
  • Equipment Room: Separate room with training equipment
  • Access: Complete Gogeta's quest to unlock

A monument to DB Asylum's history. Contains statues of all important legacy players and End of Wipe statues.

The central shopping hub of DB Asylum. The Mall Mascot NPC sits outside the building for easy Instant Transmission access.

  • Bounty Hunter — claim kill rewards & sell epic kills (see Important NPCs tab)
  • Casino & Sub Casino — accessed through the Mall
  • Combat Shop Keeper — sells techniques for UP (see Important NPCs tab)

🏠 Player House

Walk up to any available house and buy it. Houses include an open area for equipment and decorations, a gravity room, and a mob room (unlock additional mob floors as you progress).

  • Cost: 2,500 UP at 5/3
  • Legendary House Equipment: Buy from Real Estate Renji NPC (10b zenni per piece). Best gym equipment outside of Club House
  • Farm your mob floors each repop for extra EXP alongside your regular mobbing rotation
💎 Sub Room

An exclusive area available to subscribers with special training benefits.

  • Sub Gravity: Zero strain gravity (best gravity in the game)
  • Sub Med Pads: Best meditation gains available
  • Special Mob Room Access for exclusive farming zones
🍔 Wackdonalds

The in-game restaurant where you can purchase food items for training buffs and stat gains. Talk to the Cashier NPC inside.
